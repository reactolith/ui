Field

shadcn/ui docs

A form field wrapper with label, description, and error message.

Preview

Email We'll never share your email.

Usage

<ui-field>
  <ui-field-label>Email</ui-field-label>
  <ui-field-content>
    <ui-input type="email" placeholder="you@example.com"></ui-input>
  </ui-field-content>
  <ui-field-description>We'll never share your email.</ui-field-description>
</ui-field>

Sub-components

<ui-field> <ui-field-label> <ui-field-content> <ui-field-description> <ui-field-error> <ui-field-group> <ui-field-legend> <ui-field-separator> <ui-field-set> <ui-field-title>

All components also accept standard HTML attributes (class, id, style, data-*, aria-*, etc.) which are passed through to the underlying element.
